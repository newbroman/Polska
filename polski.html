🔊 Audio Setup

Polski uses your device's built-in Polish voice. Pick your device to get set up.

📌 Recommended browser: Chrome (free on Google Play)
  1. Open your phone Settings
  2. Go to General Management → Language & Input
    (or System → Languages on some phones)
  3. Tap Text-to-speech output
  4. Make sure Speech Services by Google is selected
  5. Tap the ⚙️ cog next to it
  6. Tap Install voice data
  7. Find Polski (Polska) and tap ⬇️ to download
  8. Fully close and reopen Chrome
📌 Recommended browser: Safari (built-in)
  1. Open Settings on your iPhone / iPad
  2. Go to Accessibility → Spoken Content
  3. Tap Voices
  4. Scroll down and tap Polish
  5. Tap the ☁️ cloud icon next to Zosia or Krzysztof to download
  6. Fully close Safari and reopen the app
⚠️ Check that the physical silent switch on the side of your iPhone is OFF — Apple blocks web audio in silent mode even if your volume is up.
📌 Recommended browser: Chrome or Edge

Desktop usually works automatically. If you hear no sound, try these:

  • Click anywhere on the page first — browsers block audio until you interact
  • Check your system volume is not muted
  • Firefox: Settings → Privacy → Autoplay → Allow Audio
  • Brave: Disable Shields for this site
  • Linux: Install espeak-ng and the Polish language pack
